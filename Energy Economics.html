Energy Economics

For those with interest in the many facets of the energy industry, like myself, the end goal is the development of energy sources that have the ability to provide this planet with energy in abundance. I particularly despise the phrase 'energy-intensive process', . With that being said, how exactly should one measure the abundance of energy is in our society? I believe that the best metric is the cost per unit kilowatt-hour used; if we are able to produce dirt cheap energy en masse (that is of no detriment to our environment), industries will have the freedom to go about their work without being hindered to a great extent by the electricity cost of their activities, hence abolishing the 'energy-intensive' processes. of today.
