Dismantling the Tokamak


Perhaps the greatest engineering challenge that faces man in the twenty-first century
is that of harnessing the power of the stars. If it is achieved, electricity production via
the means of nuclear fusion will bring with it countless benefits.
To some, its carbon-free nature is perhaps its most desirable property,
whilst the ample amounts of deuterium fuel that exists on our
planet also serves as a reason to pursue nuclear fusion reactors.
However, perhaps the most exciting thing about this energy source is that not only does it
produce clean electricity, but it that is has the ability to produce electricity in such abundance
never seen before by human society.
